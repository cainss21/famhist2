Cains is a very unusual surname, so I have looked for businesses, places etc called Cains. But all businesses seem to have been actually founded by a person called Cain, so the firm is really called Cain's, and then they have dropped the apostrophe. The real Cains are hard to track down on Google, because it also picks up all the Cain's, most results are "the Cain's family website" , or "John Cain's blog" etc etc. ( Of course you can see on my site that I am not a true Cains either, the name changed from Cairns or Cairens in about 1810).
